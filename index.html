What is Kpop Monkey?

Kpop Monkey is a kpop fan site created by a kpop enthusiast (me). It's a collection of my favorite kpop songs, but organized to make it fun for everyone--from experienced kpop stans, to those that are completely new. I also share my experience with kpop, and really anything else related to kpop that I'm comfortable with putting online.
